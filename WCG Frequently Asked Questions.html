Does WCG keep a record of the cards graded?

YES, for security and verification purposes, WCG keeps every serial number of each card ever graded on record.  You can easily e-mail us with a WCG serial number to verify its authenticity.

How can I confirm that WCG has received my cards?

You have three choices on how to send your cards in: U.S. Registered Mail; UPS or FEDEX.  We highly recommend that you ship cards to us with some sort of tracking number or delivery confirmation which most of our shipping choices already include.  When the cards are delivered, you will know that we received them.

What kind of holder does WCG use?

The WCG Grading holder is the finest in the industry today.  Our holders filter out much of the UV-A radiation that damages trading cards by causing photochemical changes, brittle cardboard, fading, and color shifts. WCG holders are all made of optical grade plastics, which are guaranteed to be free of impurities.  Our holders are tamper-proof with security marks hidden throughout.  After we have ultrasonically welded the holder together, it is nearly impossible to open it without causing obvious damage.  Click here for a look at our holder.

Are WCG holders Stackable?

Yes, our WCG state-of the-art, sturdy holder is the #1 stackable holder in the industry.  You will be able to store and display your cards with greater stability and less space.

What type of grading scale does WCG use?

WCG uses an easy-to-understand 1 to 10-point grading scale, with descending increments of one-half point (for example 10, 9.5, 9, 8.5, etc.).  The simplest and most accurate scale in the industry.  WCG Services will provide collectors with the finest, most thorough, consistent and accurate grading efforts available today.  Click here to view our grading scale.
